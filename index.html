Privacy Policy — JGlic

Last updated: January 17, 2026

JGlic is an application created to help users manually record their glucose levels. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle the data provided by the user.

1. Information Collected

JGlic does not automatically collect any personal information.

All information entered in the app is voluntarily typed by the user, including:

  • Glucose values (manual notes);
  • Dates and times manually entered by the user;
  • Alarms created within the app.

No data is collected through tracking, advertising, or automatic data capture.

2. Data Storage

2.1. Locally on the device

Some information may be saved directly on the user’s device.

2.2. Firebase (Google)

If the user chooses to use backup/sync resources, data will be stored in Firebase, a service provided by Google LLC.

Google Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

The developer of JGlic does not have direct access to the information stored in Firebase.

3. Data Sharing

JGlic does not share data with third parties, except when:

  • The user voluntarily uses Firebase services;
  • There is a legal requirement or court order.

4. Data Security

We use good security practices, but no system is completely immune. The user should keep their device protected.

5. Data Deletion

The user may delete their records directly within the app. If Firebase is used, deletion can be done through the Google account connected to the app.

6. Changes to This Policy

This policy may be updated occasionally. The date of the latest revision will always be visible at the top.
